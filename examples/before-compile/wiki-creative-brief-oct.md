# Creative Brief — October 2024

*This is the WIKI file BEFORE the November compile. Compare with after-compile/ to see what the compile loop actually changes.*

---

## RULES (confirmed across multiple data points)

**RULE:** Benefit-first headlines outperform curiosity-first
Evidence: Sep + Oct data. Average CVR delta: +0.8pp.

**RULE:** 25-34 women are the highest-converting segment
Evidence: 3 months consistent. CPM also lowest in this segment.

**RULE:** Retargeting outperforms prospecting by 1.5-2x ROAS
Evidence: Consistent across all months tracked.

---

## HYPOTHESES (one data point — verify next month)

**HYPOTHESIS:** Video creatives may underperform static on conversion despite higher CTR
Evidence: October data only — CTR +40% but CVR flat.

**HYPOTHESIS:** Price-focused creative underperforms benefit-focused
Evidence: October only — carousel (price focus) had 0.5x lower ROAS.

**HYPOTHESIS:** Retargeting window of 3-7 days may outperform 1-2 day window
Evidence: One campaign test in October.

---

## PATTERNS (directional — not yet rules)

- Comparison framing ("vs old way") has appeared in top 5 creatives 2 months running
- Social proof elements in top performers — "N people switched" type copy
- Outcome framing ("what happens in 30 days") showing up in winners
