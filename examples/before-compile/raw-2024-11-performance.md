## Top Converting Creatives

1. "Here's why [problem] keeps happening to you" (benefit framing) — CVR 3.2%
2. "[Product] vs the old way" (comparison) — CVR 2.9%
3. "[Number] people switched this month" (social proof) — CVR 2.7%
4. "What happens in 30 days" (outcome framing) — CVR 2.5%
5. "You won't believe this works" (curiosity) — CVR 1.4%

## Audience Notes

- 25-34 women: highest CVR (3.4%), lowest CPM
- 35-44 women: high AOV but lower CVR (2.1%)
- Men 18-34: lowest CVR (0.8%), high bounce
- Retargeting (3-day window): 4.8x ROAS vs 3.2x prospecting
- Lookalike 1% (from purchasers): best prospecting segment

## Notes from Session

- Video CTR is 50% higher than static but CVR is actually similar or lower
- Curiosity hooks consistently underperform benefit-first framing
- Price-focused creative shows lower ROAS than benefit-focused — hypothesis: buyers want the outcome, not the discount
- Retargeting window sweet spot seems to be 3-7 days, not 1-2 days
