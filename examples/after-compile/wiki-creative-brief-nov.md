## RULES (confirmed across multiple data points)

**RULE:** Benefit-first headlines outperform curiosity-first
Evidence: Sep + Oct + Nov data. Average CVR delta: +1.1pp. Nov reinforced.
*Nov note: Curiosity hooks appeared in bottom 2 performers again — now confident this is a reliable signal.*

**RULE:** Video drives CTR, not conversion — use for top-of-funnel only
Evidence: Nov confirmed Oct hypothesis. Video CTR +50% vs static, CVR flat or lower.
*Practical: Use video for prospecting/awareness. Use static for retargeting and conversion objectives.*
*Upgraded from HYPOTHESIS → RULE (two data points confirming)*

**RULE:** Benefit-first, outcome-focused copy outperforms price-focused copy
Evidence: Nov confirmed Oct hypothesis. Static (benefit) 4.35x ROAS vs carousel (price) 3.59x.
*Practical: Lead with what the buyer gets, not what they save.*
*Upgraded from HYPOTHESIS → RULE (two data points confirming)*

**RULE:** 25-34 women are the highest-converting segment
Evidence: 4 months consistent. CPM also lowest. Best for conversion campaigns.

**RULE:** Retargeting 3-7 day window outperforms 1-2 day window
Evidence: Nov confirmed Oct hypothesis. 4.8x ROAS at 3-day vs prior 1-day tests.
*Upgraded from HYPOTHESIS → RULE (two data points confirming)*
